Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered

Is MediBuddy AI accurate?
MediBuddy AI analyzes 378 diseases and 377 symptoms with 95% match accuracy. However, it's not a replacement for professional medical advice. Always consult a doctor for serious symptoms.
Can I trust the medicine recommendations?
Medicine recommendations come from a verified medical database. However, always follow your doctor's prescriptions, as individual needs vary based on allergies, other conditions, and medications.
Does it replace a doctor?
No. MediBuddy AI is a health information tool to help you understand symptoms better. For diagnosis, treatment, or any health concerns, always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
Is my data private?
Your diagnosis history is stored securely in your personal account. We don't share data with third parties. Only you can see your health dashboard.
Can I save my diagnosis history?
Yes! Create an account to automatically save every diagnosis. View trends, charts, and your complete health history in your personal dashboard.
What's the difference between severity levels?
Severity (1-5) helps the AI weight symptoms: 1 = mild/occasional, 5 = severe/constant. This improves diagnosis accuracy.
